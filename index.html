🤖 Under The Hood

The 6 Adaptive Rules

Transparent intelligence — you can always see why the system makes decisions

R1

Remedial Focus

Score below 60%? Get easier questions in that topic until your mastery improves.

Threshold: < 60%
R2

Progressive Challenge

3 correct answers in a row? The difficulty level automatically increases.

Trigger: 3-streak
R3

Mastery Reinforcement

Scored above 80%? Topics are reviewed less often using spaced repetition.

Threshold: > 80%
R4

Prerequisite Validation

Advanced topics only unlock after 50%+ mastery of required foundational topics.

Min Mastery: ≥ 50%
R5

Cross-Domain Integration

Strong in one topic? Related concepts from other subjects are introduced automatically.

Threshold: > 70%
R6

Balanced Practice

Sessions always mix recall, application, and analysis for well-rounded learning.

Based on: Bloom's Taxonomy
